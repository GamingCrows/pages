Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 8, 2026

Introduction

Gaming Crows ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Authentication Data: We use third-party providers (Google and Apple) for user authentication. When you sign in using these services, we receive your email address, display name, and a unique user ID. We need this data strictly to create and verify your account, enable cross-device cloud saves, and manage player leaderboards.
  • Game Progress: We store your level completions, achievements, and preferences to sync across devices.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for analytics and ad personalization.
  • Usage Data: In-game actions, session duration, and level completion statistics to improve game experience.
  • Crash Reports: Technical information about app crashes to help us fix bugs.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the App
  • Sync game progress across your devices
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance gameplay
  • Respond to your requests and support needs

Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob

We display advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may collect and use data to personalize ads. Learn more: Google Privacy Policy

Firebase (Google)

We use Firebase for:

  • Authentication: Sign-in with Google/Apple
  • Cloud Firestore: Cloud save for game progress
  • Analytics: Usage statistics (optional)
  • Crashlytics: Crash reporting

Learn more: Firebase Privacy

Your Choices

Ad Personalization

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking

Account Data

You can request deletion of your account data by contacting us. Game progress stored locally on your device can be cleared by uninstalling the App.

Account Deletion

You can delete your GridLock account and all associated data using either of the following methods:

In-App Deletion:

  1. Open GridLock
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Data Retention

We retain your authentication data and game progress for as long as you maintain an active account with us to provide seamless cloud saves and leaderboards. If you choose to delete your account, we will permanently delete all associated data (including your email, display name, user ID, and game progress) from our servers within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Our App is suitable for all ages but is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by posting the new policy in the App with an updated effective date.
